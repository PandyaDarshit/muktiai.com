About Mukti AI Academy

Our mission is to democratize AI education and empower individuals and organizations for the future

Our Story

Mukti AI Academy was founded with a vision to make AI education accessible and practical for everyone. The name "Mukti" comes from the Sanskrit word meaning "liberation," reflecting our belief that true knowledge liberates and empowers.

Our motto "सा विद्या या विमुक्तये" (Sa Vidya Ya Vimuktaye) encapsulates our core philosophy - "True knowledge is that which liberates." We believe that understanding and mastering AI technology can liberate individuals and organizations from traditional constraints and open new possibilities for growth and innovation.

Meet Our Founder

Academic Background

With a Master's degree in Computational Mechanics and a Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering, our founder brings a unique perspective to AI education, combining theoretical knowledge with practical applications.

Professional Experience

Since 2019, they have been working in computer science, focusing on AI implementation and education. Their experience spans across various industries, helping organizations and individuals navigate the AI revolution.

Vision

Passionate about the potential of AI to transform society, they established Mukti AI Academy in 2025 to help bridge the gap between AI technology and its practical implementation.
